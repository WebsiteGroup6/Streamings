A good stream is about keeping the viewers entertained and engaged. The characteristics of a good stream are:

Interaction - Streamers who respond to chats, answer questions, and involve the audience can keep them coming back.
Personality - Viewers appreciate streamers who are respectful, genuine, and approachable. Showing your real personality makes the stream feel more personal and keeps people coming back for more.
